Kavan TheKid

Kavan Cardoza aka Kavan TheKid first became a Conceptual/Surrealist photographer in 2014.
He started his exploration with a 365 project, after stumbling into Self Portrait Photography. The 365 project became a source of therapy for Kavan, with each photo carrying a heavy emotion.
Over the course of the next few years, Kavan beat his first serious bout of depression and his technical skillset as a photographer developed further.
In 2018, he had hit rock bottom. His work had lesser emotions put into them as usual, as it felt tiresome for him. He was not happy with who he was or where he was going. That's when he decided to start his intense road to recovery, with the support of his family and friends.
In 2019, he made a promise to only produce the most personal and highest quality work he could create. He also started to work on his first photography book "Stay Inside & Don't Let The Dreams Out", which is out for sale now. Buy book!
He also got nominated and won awards such as:
Digitally Enhanced Photography - 1st Place (International Photography Awards (IPAs)) Stocksy United Photography Awards - Top 25 Finalist (2019 Beautiful Bizarre Art Prize)


Kavan TheKid's message to people struggling with mental health issues:
First and foremost you are not alone. There is always someone who cares for you. There is always someone who wants you here and who is willing to talk to you. Second, don’t be afraid to admit you need help. It’s probably the hardest thing to do and requires great strength. But each and every single person who is experiencing these emotions or feelings has that strength inside them.
